This is a wonderful list of the amazing books I have read this year.

The weight of the heart

This is a story about a female android that is not like the rest. She was implanted with a full set of memories at birth and she is terrified of dying.
However she must overcome her fears in order to do her job as a private investigator and save the lives of others. The worst part is that she can only live for 10 years.
